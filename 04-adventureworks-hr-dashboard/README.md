# AdventureWorks HR & Employee Analytics Dashboard

**Tools:** Power BI | DAX | HR Analytics | Data Modeling

## Overview
A 5-page Power BI dashboard analyzing workforce composition, compensation, and sales team performance using the AdventureWorks HR dataset.

## Dataset Scope
- 290 employees
- 13 departments

## What Was Done
- Modeled relationships across Employee, Department History, Pay History, Person, SalesPerson, and Sales Order tables to enable cross-functional HR and sales analysis.
- Created DAX measures including Total Employees, Average Tenure, Total Annual Payroll Estimate, Latest Pay Rate, and Quota Achievement %.

## Key Insights
- Average employee tenure: 16.98 years.
- Total annual payroll estimate: $10.97M.
- Production accounts for 62% of headcount but earns among the lowest hourly rates ($13.54/hr) compared to Engineering/IT ($33-34/hr).
- Top salesperson (Mitchell) generates $11.7M in sales, vs. a team average of $7.25M per rep.
